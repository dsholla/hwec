Where, how?

Where are you located?

Hounslow West Evangelical Church is located in west London, a few miles east of Heathrow Airport. We are a few minutes walk from Hounslow West tube station which is on the Piccadilly Line. The M4 (junction 3) is about 5 to 10 minutes drive away (depending on the traffic).

Enter your address for directions:

Example: 10 Downing Street, London, SW1A 2AA

How can I get there?

Getting to us by road

Hounslow West Evangelical Church is in west London, and is located approximately 2 miles from Junction 3 of the M4. The map above shows where the church is located.

HWEC car park

Please note that it is not possible to turn right into Vicarage Farm Road from the eastbound carriageway of the A4 (Great West Road). If approaching from the west, it is necessary to take the A3006 (Bath Road) at the junction of the A4, A3006 and A30, and then turn left into Vicarage Farm Road at the first set of traffic lights. The church is a couple of hundred yards down the road on the left on the corner of Vicarage Farm Road and Renfrew Road.

Note: on site car parking available for regular services.

Reaching us by rail

Hounslow West Evangelical Church is just a few hundred yards from Hounslow West tube station on the Piccadilly Line. Turn right out of the station, right again at the traffic lights into Vicarage Farm Road (at the junction of Bath Road with Vicarage Farm Road and Rosemary Avenue). The church is a couple of hundred yards down the road on the left on the corner of Vicarage Farm Road and Renfrew Road.
